Top 5 Tourist Spots Near Pagadian City

Dao-Dao Islands

Dao-Dao Islands are twin white-sand islands located in Pagadian City. Known for crystal-clear waters, snorkeling areas, and relaxing beaches, this destination is perfect for weekend island hopping and family outings.

  • Location: Pagadian City
  • Estimated Travel Time: 20–30 minutes by boat from Pagadian Port
  • Best Activities: Swimming, snorkeling, beach camping

Mount Timolan

Mount Timolan is a protected natural park offering scenic hiking trails, cool mountain air, and panoramic views of Zamboanga del Sur. It is a favorite destination for adventure seekers and nature lovers.

  • Location: Tigbao, Zamboanga del Sur
  • Estimated Travel Time: 1 hour from Pagadian City
  • Best Activities: Hiking, trekking, photography

Tuboran Eriberta Spring

Tuboran Eriberta Spring is a natural cold spring located in Barangay Tuboran, Mahayag, Zamboanga del Sur. It is known for its crystal-clear and refreshing water that flows continuously from the mountains, making it a perfect destination for relaxation and family outings.

  • Location: Tuburan, Mahayag, Zamboanga del Sur
  • Estimated Travel Time: 45 minutes to 1 hour from Pagadian City
  • Best Activities: Swimming in natural cold spring wate, Family picnics and gatherings, Relaxation and nature photography, Barkada outings

Pulacan Falls

Pulacan Falls is a beautiful hidden waterfall surrounded by greenery. It is a refreshing getaway perfect for swimming and relaxing in nature.

  • Location: Labangan, Zamboanga del Sur
  • Estimated Travel Time: 45 minutes from Pagadian City
  • Best Activities: Swimming, trekking, nature trips

Pagadian City Boulevard

Pagadian City Boulevard is one of the most popular leisure and recreational areas in Pagadian City, Zamboanga del Sur. Located along the coastal area facing Illana Bay, the boulevard offers a relaxing seaside atmosphere perfect for walking, jogging, and spending quality time with family and friends.

  • Location: Pagadian City, Zamboanga del Sur
  • Estimated Travel Time: Approximately 5–10 minutes (accessible within the city)
  • Best Activities: Sunset viewing, Jogging and walking, Family bonding and picnics, Street food trips, Photography
